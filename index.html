Our Story

More Than Just
a Café

Mariam Juice Cafe was born from a simple belief: that everyone deserves a sip of something truly fresh. Founded in Hailakandi, Assam, MJC is the town's go-to destination for premium, handcrafted beverages.

We source the freshest seasonal fruits daily, refuse to use artificial flavours, and pour every glass with pride. What started as a neighbourhood café has grown into Hailakandi's most beloved juice destination.
