Our Story

More Than Just
a Café

Mariam Juice Cafe was born from a simple belief: that everyone deserves a sip of something truly fresh. Founded in Hailakandi, Assam, MJC is the town's go-to destination for premium, handcrafted beverages.

We source the freshest seasonal fruits daily, refuse to use artificial flavours, and pour every glass with pride. What started as a neighbourhood café has grown into Hailakandi's most beloved juice destination.

  • 🍃

    100% Fresh, Zero Preservatives

    Every drink is prepared fresh to order using locally sourced, seasonal produce.

  • ✨

    Premium Quality Standards

    Our hygiene and quality processes match the standard of upscale cafés anywhere.

  • 💛

    Made with Love for Hailakandi

    We're proud to be a local brand serving our community with heart and passion.

  • 🏆

    Best in Town — by Choice

    Recognised by thousands of happy customers as Hailakandi's #1 cafe destination.
